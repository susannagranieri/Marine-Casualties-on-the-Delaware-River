Where Large Vessels Experienced Marine Casualties on the Delaware River
Locations of United States Coast Guard Incident Investigation Activities/Reports into Reportable Marine Casualties (RMC) occurring in the Sector Delaware Bay (SDB) Area of Responsibility (AOR) from 2013 to 2024, involving Vessels over 400 feet in length, wherein the vessel encountered a loss/reduction or propulsion/steering OR a loss of electrical power.
